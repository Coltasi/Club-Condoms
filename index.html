Frequently Asked Questions

We get it. You have questions. We have answers. Most are appropriate.

Will it fit my Big Bertha? ▶
Yes. Our flexible, self-expanding polymer blend accommodates oversized heads without tearing, splitting, or making you feel embarrassed at the register. We've tested it on the biggest drivers on the market. Your Big Bertha is safe with us.
How do I put one on for the first time? ▶
Open the individual wrapper, pinch the tip, and roll it smoothly down the shaft from the head toward the grip in one confident motion. Our free illustrated Installation Guide covers every club type in detail. Do not rush. Rushing causes tearing. This is true of many things in life.
Are they reusable? ▶
Technically yes. We don't recommend it. For peak performance and basic dignity, single-use per round is strongly advised. At $20.95 for 24, you can absolutely afford a fresh one every time. Treat yourself. You're worth it. Your clubs are worth it.
Can I use Club Condoms in the rain? ▶
Absolutely. Our Vibration-Damping Polymer is fully moisture-resistant. Wet conditions are actually where Club Condoms perform best — the material maintains grip and form even when everything else on the course is getting slippery. You stay in total control.
What exactly makes the Putter Poncho different? ▶
The Putter Poncho provides full-length coverage — shaft AND head — in one continuous piece. Unlike a standard cover that just sits on top, the Poncho goes all the way down. It's softer, thinner, and offers a more intimate level of protection. Putter people understand. You'll understand once you try it.
