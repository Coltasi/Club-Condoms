Will it fit my Big Bertha? ▶
Yes. Our flexible, self-expanding polymer blend accommodates oversized heads without tearing. Your Big Bertha is safe with us.
How do I put one on for the first time? ▶
Open the wrapper, pinch the tip, and roll smoothly down the shaft in one confident motion. Do not rush. Rushing causes tearing. This is true of many things in life.
Are they reusable? ▶
Technically yes. We don't recommend it. Single-use per round is strongly advised. At $20.95 for 24, you can afford a fresh one every time. You're worth it.
Can I use Club Condoms in the rain? ▶
Absolutely. Our Vibration-Damping Polymer is fully moisture-resistant. Wet conditions are where Club Condoms perform best. You stay in total control.
